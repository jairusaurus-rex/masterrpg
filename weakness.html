Book 1: Core

If anything forces you to select a weakness, you MUST choose a weakness you do not have already. If there are no more to choose from, your stamina decreases by 2 as though you took “Sickly” again.

Lame. Your speed is reduced by 10. If on foot, your travel speed is cut in half.

Weak Vision. To you, all normal light becomes dim light, dim light becomes darkness, darkness becomes Advanced Darkness, Advance Darkness is impossible for you to see even with other effects helping you.

Slow learner. Your total Proficiency is decreased by 5

Sickly Your stamina is decreased equal by 2

Fragile Your health is decreased by 10

One Arm  If you are down to one arm, no feature can give you more, you cannot hold an item that requires 2 hands. Your Large Carry decreases by 1

Coward Every time an enemy causes you to take damage, you must make a bravery skill check against the damage plus ten. If you fail, you are frightened of that enemy. Every turn after you can take that check again to get rid of the frightened condition

Clumsy  Your Large carry and small carry decreases by 2. Every time you roll a natural 1, you drop whatever is in your hands.

Tired Every time you take an amount of levels of exhaustion, you gain one more level of exhaustion. It takes one more hour of sleep to gain a rest.

Crazy While targeting an enemy with anything hostel and you roll a natural one, you must target an ally instead. If that is impossible, you target yourself

Mute: You cannot speak verbally

  11. You are frightened by the nearest creature until the end of your next turn
  12. You are disoriented
