Clothing

You can buy Clothing for 1 gold piece and it adds 10 to your small carry capacity.

Without clothing Bartering, Charm, Converse, Convince, Deceive, Disguise, Intimidate Leadership are all nulled. If you don’t buy clothing, you can still be assumed to be wearing simple tunics or undergarments that don’t count as any weight but gives you no small carry load and still null Bartering, Charm, Converse, Convince, Deceive, Disguise, Intimidate Leadership

Clothing must be equipped as a large carry load to work.

Though any clothing weighing 1 large can be put into the small carry load as 5 small weights, however you cannot add to your small carry capacity with any clothing stored this way.

You can only wear one of these at a time, though you can have more on your person, however, clothing that you are caring about and not wearing does not add small carry capacity.

You customize your clothing by adding from the list below. You can add each item more than once to get more of its effect, however you must add its cost each time you add it.

Concealed Pockets: Gain a buff to sleight of hand or deceive when hiding half the small capacity your clothing gives you. You can only add this to your clothing twice. If you get this Cost 10 extra gold

Fine clothing: Gain a buff point for bartering, charm, converse, convenience, desciece and leadership to anyone who has a positive view of wealthy or noble people. Cost 20 extra gold

It has pockets: Gain 10 more small carry capacity. This can be only added to your clothing once. Cost 10 extra gold.

Warm clothing: When wearing, reduce a degree of cold climate but if there is one or more degree of heat, wearing this increases the degree by 2. Cost 10 extra gold and weight increase by 1 large weight

Brimmed Hatwear: Provides some shade. Cost 10 extra gold.

Poncho/cape: Provides protection from getting wet from rain. Cost 10 extra gold.

Shrouded Hood: Provides a buff against being recognized. Cost 10 extra gold.

Armor

What armor you wear add buffs to your Armor skill check.

While wearing armor, the dodge, reflex, stealth, climb, and swim are all Nulled.

You cannot dash while wearing armor

It takes several minutes to put on and remove armor. The amount of minutes needed is equal to the Resistance plus 1

Though any armor weighing 1 large can be put into the small carry load as 5 small weights, however you cannot use any armor stored this way.

You can only wear one armor at a time

While wearing armor, you can count it as clothing to no longer have Bartering, Charm, Converse, Convince, Deceive, Disguise, Intimidate Leadership nulled. You can still wear one armor and one clothing at the same time to gain the benefits of both

Weapons

Weapons use different skill checks to try to do damage to an enemy. If an attack is successful, it does the damage to the enemy according to the weapons damage. You can also use a strike skill to try to do 2 damage using your fists, feet, or other natural attacks.

Though any weapon weighing 1 large can be put into the small carry load as 5 small weights, however you cannot attack with any weapon stored this way.

Goods

Goods can be bought at one town and sold at  a different town. Like all items, the GM can rule that a certain amount of  a good is offered at a town. The GM can change the weight and price for gameplay but they are ruled here as 10 gold each for a box weighing 1 large weight.

When selling the goods, you use the barter skill. If the good has no purpose in that town or there is no market place, the barter skill is nulled. The offer for the good is ((roll - Good DC) /100) + original price. In other words, the percentage difference above or below the good DC.
