How to make a character

  1. Concept: Talk to your GM about what books are allowed and what the setting is. Then create a character concept that would fit that settings
  2. Find Starting Level: Talk to your GM about what the starting Level is and therefore what your Character Bonus is. From there you can calculate all other parts of your character. The bonus is 2 plus one every fifth level.
  3. Set Ability Scores: With the point buy system, determine your character’s Strength, Dexterity, Grit, Focus, Intelligence, Wit, Charisma, and Aura
  4. Calculate Stats: With the ability scores, calculate all your character’s stats
  5. Choose Trained Skills: Select which skills your character is proficient in. Your character’s Stats shows how many you can choose
  6. Choose Feats: Select which feats your character has. Your character’s Stats shows the total number of feat tiers you can choose
  7. Guy Gear: Buy gear for your characters. Your character’s Stats show your character’s carry load and starting wealth.
  8. Select Spells: If your character can learn spells, you can add any spells up to your total spells known, which is again, shown in your stats. Note: this is only for games that involve books that have magic.
