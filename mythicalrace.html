Pick either hot or cold. Reduce the climate degree by the tier of what you chose but for every 2 tiers, the one you did not chose will increase by 1 if you are in a climate of at least 1 degree.

Energy Touched ___

Tier 1-7

Pick one type of energy (see Magic rules: types of damage). Gain resistance from any damage with that energy. Any magic that uses that element gains buff to any skill check equal to the Tier. If that spell does damage, it does additional damage equal to your tier.  You gain an energy pool equal to your bonus. Every used item (touch blast attack, breath weapon, retaliation reaction, energy body) uses one energy pool. You regain your energy pool after a rest.

Tier 2-4, pick one of the following, touch blast attack, breath weapon, retaliation reaction.

touch blast attack: as a minor action, Add *A to the damage of a strike attack

breath weapon: As a Major action, from your position, make a 15 foot cone attack, your Magic Burst Check versus Reflex Check. If you win, they take *A damage, if they win, they take half damage.

retaliation reaction: As a reaction, When someone attacks you with a melee attack and succeed, you can send out your energy to hurt them as well. Your Magic Burst Check versus Reflex Check. If you win, they take *A damage, if they win, they take half damage.

Starting at Tier 5, as a Major action you can transform your body into your element.  This is your energy body.

Energy Body: While in this state you gain the following: This is your energy body. You gain a resistance to weapon damage equal to the tier. You can pass through object that anything two sizes small can pass through like bars or chains. You can use retaliation reaction without using a reaction and without using your energy pool.

At Tier 5 it last one round, at Tier 6 it last one minute You can end it early as a minor action but it still counts as one use, at Tier 7 it last one hour. You can end it early  as a minor action but it still counts as one use

Gains another limb for each Tier: an arm, a tail or something else. You can hold a weapon or shield in those new limbs. All limbs can draw and sheath weapons using the same action. You gain one Large carry capacity for each limb. You do not gain any attacks with these limbs.

Tier 1, pick either the Climb, Swim, Athletics, Acrobatics. You can add *A as a secondary ability to the skill. Each Tier past the second you man choose another skill from that list and add *A as a secondary skill to that skill.

Size Difference

Choose Small or Large

Small: You can add *A as a secondary ability to Stealth, Dodge, and Armor. You can disengage the enemy as a minor action. Any skill that has *S as its primary ability is nulled. All weapons and strike damages do one less damage. You have 5 less health.

Large: Any skill that has *D as its primary ability is Nulled. All weapons and strike damages do one additional damage. You have 5 more health. You gain a Melee reach of ten feet and threaten a space at ten feet. You must take a full move action to fit through normal doors and walk at half speed through rooms that are made for medium size races and/or humans.

If any Poisons and magical effects triest to put you to sleep, you get a buff on any skill that avoids it. The bluff number is twice the Tier.

Tier 1-2

Tail

Wings Tier 1-5

Tier 1 gives you wings that are vestigial and just for show. Tier 2 lets you glide with your winds, allowing for a slow fall that causes only half of the fall damage you take. Tier 3 lets you glide with your winds, allowing for a slow fall that does not cause damage. Tier 4 allows you to flew at half hour speed for an hour.
