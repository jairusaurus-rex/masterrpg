Abilities and Stats


There are 8 ability scores in the game. They aid different skills and are needed to gain some feats. You have points for abilities equal to 20 plus your bonus plus your level. You can save some of these points in reserve or use them once you level up to level up any of those 8 abilities. You can only transfer from reserve to upgrade ability scores when you level up.

Each upgrade ability has a cost. By default, the cost is the number you are going to. If you have a strength of 5 you need to spend 6 points in your ability reserve to bring your strength to 6. This makes it easy to have low ability scores but hard to have high ability scores.

When levelling up, you can upgrade any number of ability scores and upgrade them any to any amount as long as you can afford each change form you reserve.

For example, at level one, you have 23 in reserve but if you were making a level 6 character you would have 26. Book 1: Core

When you level up, your stats will change (such as your health or carry capacity). Stats will change when you level up, gain more character bonuses, gain feats, or change your ability score. The default of those calculations are found below

Max Hit Points = 10 + level + G * Bonus

Stamina Points = G + Bonus

Max Large Carry = 2*S + G + Bonus

Max Small = Bonus + what your large items allow.

Max Magical Item = A + Bonus

Speed: 20 + *D + bonus

Dark vision = F*5

Starting wealth = Level * Bonus * 50 + 10 * (I+W+C)
