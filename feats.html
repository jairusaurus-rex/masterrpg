With any instrument you are proficient in, you can use a major action to start your bardic performance. You can use a minor action each turn after to continue it as long as you are still holding your instrument throwout your turn. You can end it as a free action.

It takes two hands to play an instrument. You cannot hold any weapon or other object, but you can take a hand off your instrument for a moment to perform other tasks other than holding an object. Examples are casting spells, drinking medicine, or punching.

You can use bardic performance for the number of turns per day equal to *C plus your Bonus.

While performing, until your next turn, any alley that can hear your performance gains a buff on all skill checks equal to your bardic performance tier.
